HOW TO PLAY

  • Shoot Fireworks: Click or tap anywhere in the sky to launch interceptor rockets.
  • Defend City: Use the explosion blast radius to destroy incoming meteors before they hit the city!
  • Dome Shield: Destroy meteors to charge your shield. Press SPACE or click Shield button when full for invincibility!
  • Boss Battles: Every 5 waves an Armored Boss Meteor spawns! Boss has 70% rocket damage reduction and spawns sub-meteors every 5 seconds.
  • Rocket Ammunition: You start with 100 rockets. Defeating a Boss rewards you with +50 rockets stock!
